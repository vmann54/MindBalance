Common Forms of Therapy

Different therapy approaches help with different needs. Here are a few:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) – Focuses on changing negative thought patterns.
  • Talk Therapy – General counseling through open conversation with a licensed therapist.
  • Group Therapy – Provides support through shared experiences with others.
  • Teletherapy – Accessible mental health care from the comfort of your home.
